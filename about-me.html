About Me

Hi, I'm M. Mekonen

I am a Computational Engineer with over 18 years of experience in Finite Element (FE) Simulation, method development, and automation.

My work spans across multiple industries, including Automotive, Aerospace, and Energy. I specialize in improving the efficiency and accuracy of multiphysics simulations, developing innovative approaches for the integration of AI and Machine Learning (ML) in engineering workflows.

Key Areas of Expertise

  • FE Simulation for complex systems (Automotive, Aerospace, etc.)
  • Method Development for process automation in pre/post processing
  • Co-Simulation techniques for advanced modeling and behavior characterization
  • Machine Learning and AI integration for simulation optimization

My Journey

I started my academic journey with a B.Sc. in Civil Engineering from Mekelle University, Ethiopia. I then continued my education in Germany, earning an M.Sc. in Computational Engineering in Science from the Technical University of Braunschweig, and later a Dr.-Ing. from the Helmholtz-Center-Geesthacht & University of Kiel.

My career began with the German Aerospace Center (DLR), where I focused on the numerical investigation of CFRP structures. From there, I expanded into more complex domains like the Automotive industry, working on safety systems and interior components for Volkswagen, Porsche, and others.

Currently, I am exploring new frontiers in AI-driven simulation automation and how machine learning can enhance simulation processes.

  • Preprocessors: ANSA, Hypermesh
  • Programming: Python, C, C++, C#, Fortran, Java
